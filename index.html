    Our History

    • Founded in 2021 by four undergraduate students from Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology, SOCIAL is driven by the aim of creating social impact using technology. We organize bootcamps, networking sessions, and national social impact product pitches so as to provide social entrepreneurs with a platform to network, learn and validate their solutions. 

    • We have collaborated with consulting agencies, social entrepreneurs, and startups to foster innovation and social impact across India. Our journey is fueled by a commitment to empowering social entrepreneurs and creating sustainable solutions to address societal challenges. 
    • Official Selection- Millennium Fellowship Class ‘21 & Class ’22

    Our Mission

    • Our project SOCIAL aims at creating employers rather than employees; by providing a support system to social impact startups.

      We connect them to the right resources and expertise, thus creating an enabling environment for ideas to grow and develop with emphasis on products which solve problems faced by the society at large.
