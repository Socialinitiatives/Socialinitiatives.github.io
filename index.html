    Our History

    • Founded in 2021 by four undergraduate students, SOCIAL is driven by the aim of creating social impact using technology. We organize bootcamps, networking sessions, and national social impact pitches so as to provide social entrepreneurs & social innovators with a platform to network, learn & validate their solutions. 

    • We have collaborated with consulting agencies, social entrepreneurs, and startups to foster innovation and social impact across India. Our journey is fueled by a commitment to empowering social entrepreneurs & social innovators to create sustainable solutions to address societal challenges. 
    • Official Selection- Millennium Fellowship Class ‘21 & Class ’22

    The IEEE Kochi Subsection is the IEEE organisation which covers Central region of Kerala. The subsection aims to utilize and organize this technical strength towards advancing technology for humanity. .

    IEEE SB RSET

    The IEEE Student Branch Of Rajagiri School Of Engineering And Technology Is the product of a combined effort from the qualified faculty members & talented students Of RSET. We are a gathering of energetic & innovative individuals from various disciplines of engineering. 

    180 DEGREES

    180 Degrees Consulting NSUT is a student-run consulting agency which provides services to NGO's & social impact ventures. They have been the pillar of support for SOCIAL & been with us since our founding
