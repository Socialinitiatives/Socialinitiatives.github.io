Our History

Our History

  • Our team is comprised of four undergraduate students from Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology who are working together to bring about social change in society that too often leaves entrepreneurs and social supports behind. We aim to give priority to social entrepreneurship and social support in order to bring about a change in the way entrepreneurship is perceived.

Our History

Our History

Our History

  • Our social project was extremely successful last year. We conducted bootcamps, online training sessions, and national level competitions, and were selected as a United Nations Millennium Fellowship project for 2021. We have a very wide reach and were able to get many social entrepreneurs on board. We received sponsorship from multiple companies and it was a very successful year for us.

Our Mission

Our History

Our Mission

  • Our project SOCIAL, aims at creating employers rather than employees; by motivating and providing a support system to students with innovative ideas to bring out and try out their ideas. We connect them to the right resources and expertise, thus creating an enabling environment for ideas and students to grow and develop. We aim to achieve this goal through a Product Pitch Competition with emphasis on products which solve problems faced by the society at large.
