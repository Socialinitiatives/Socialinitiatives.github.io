    Our History

    • SOCIAL was founded in 2021 by 4 undergrad students of Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology with an aim to bring social impact.

      In 2021 we organized bootcamps, online training & networking sessions, and a national social impact product pitch, and were selected for Millennium Fellowship class '21.

      We have a very wide reach and were able to get many social entrepreneurs & startups on board for our programs and support system.

    Our Mission

    • Our project SOCIAL aims at creating employers rather than employees; by providing a support system to social impact startups.

      We connect them to the right resources and expertise, thus creating an enabling environment for ideas to grow and develop with emphasis on products which solve problems faced by the society at large.

    OUR Partners

    Millennium fellowship

    The Millennium Fellowship is a semester-long leadership development program that happens on campus across the globe. The United Nations Academic Impact and MCN are the organizers of the program. 

    SOCIAL is an official selection to the class of '21 & '22 Millennium Fellowship

    RSET IEDC

    RSET IEDC is a Government of Kerala sponsored entrepreneurship development cell under Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) 

    With Over 500+ members within the colleges, and has extensive contacts with other entrepreneurship development cells across the state and outside. 

    RSET IEDC is amongst the best performing IEDC's in the state and has won multiple  accolades .

    Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology

    Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology (RSET) - Autonomous, Kakkanad, Kochi, is a premier educational institution offering excellence in engineering education and research. The University Grants Commission has granted autonomous status to RSET from 2020 onwards.
